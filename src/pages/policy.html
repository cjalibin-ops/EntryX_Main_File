Policy

Policy of the University of Northern Philippines

focuses on producing globally competitive, ethical graduates through quality instruction, research, and community service, emphasizing good governance, cultural preservation, gender inclusivity, and modern technology, with specific rules like mandatory 80% class attendance for students and modernizing facilities for digital learning.

VISION

A Globally Recognized university in a Heritage City by 2030.

MISSION

To produce globally skilled and morally upright professionals instilled with rich cultural values.
